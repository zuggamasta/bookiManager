Convert Audio for booki

Convert any audio file to be played with booki stickers.

This is a community project. Use it at your own risk!

  1. Record the audio on a device of your choice.
  2. Drag and drop your audio file. (the files are not uploaded to the internet and stay on your computer)
  3. After the conversion is done, a button with link will enable you to download your file.
  4. Copy the files to to the booki pen via USB into the diyRecord folder.
